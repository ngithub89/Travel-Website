The History of Gorkha

Ancient Times

Gorkha has been inhabited since ancient times, with historical artifacts dating back to the Licchavi period.

Unification of Nepal

In the 18th century, King Prithvi Narayan Shah began his campaign to unify Nepal from Gorkha.

Gorkha Durbar

The Gorkha Durbar, a majestic palace, was built in the 18th century and stands as a symbol of Gorkha's royal history.

Modern Development

In recent years, Gorkha has developed into a popular tourist destination, known for its rich history and beautiful landscapes.
